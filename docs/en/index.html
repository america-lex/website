Invitation

We invite you to participate in the foundation of the Association of Lexicography for the Americas. Individuals, institutions, and publishing houses from Central and South America, the Caribbean and Mexico are welcome to participate, and any person or body with interest in the lexicography of this region is also welcome to join regardless of their home or working country.
